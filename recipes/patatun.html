Patatun

What is patatun?

Patatun is a healthy dish that is quick to make and only requires 4 ingredients.

It's a jacket spud filled with a creamy cheese tomato sauce mixed with tuna for extra protein!

Ingredients

  • 1 big potato
  • 300ml tomato sauce
  • 2 spoons of cream cheese
  • 1 large tin of tuna

Steps

  1. First, cook your spud, baked, boiled, or microwaved
  2. Secondly, empty the sauce into a pan and add the cream cheese
  3. Then, add the tuna to the sauce and reduce until thick
  4. lastly, serve the sauce onto the potato and enjoy!
